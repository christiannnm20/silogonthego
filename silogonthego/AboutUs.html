About Us

Silog On The Go is a small fast food chain located in #18 Fendler st. East Tapinac Olongapo City, Zambales, Philippines. We started in March 2019 in the midst of pandemic, We offer local, seasonal and popular Filipino's breakfast menu. Our Long-standing relationships with local farmers ensure that we will bring the best of the market to your table. A sustainable philosophy is at the heart of everything we do, We're available for Delivery,Dine In and Take-out. We also serve beer beverages and you can sing all night in our videoke from 6pm-6am and we're open 24/7 Monday-Sunday.
